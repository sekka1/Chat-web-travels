# Japan Travel Guide

Japan is a fascinating destination that blends ancient traditions with cutting-edge technology. From bustling cities to serene temples, Japan offers a unique travel experience.

## Best Time to Visit

- **Spring (March-May)**: Cherry blossom season, pleasant weather
- **Fall (September-November)**: Autumn foliage, comfortable temperatures
- **Winter (December-February)**: Skiing, hot springs, fewer tourists
- **Summer (June-August)**: Festival season, but hot and humid

## Top Destinations

### Tokyo
The vibrant capital city with a mix of modern and traditional attractions:
- Shibuya Crossing and Harajuku for urban culture
- Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa
- Tokyo Skytree for panoramic views
- Tsukiji Outer Market for fresh seafood

### Kyoto
Ancient capital with over 2,000 temples and shrines:
- Fushimi Inari Shrine with thousands of orange torii gates
- Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)
- Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
- Gion district for geisha culture

### Osaka
Food lover's paradise and entertainment hub:
- Dotonbori for street food and nightlife
- Osaka Castle
- Universal Studios Japan
- Kuromon Ichiba Market

### Mount Fuji
Japan's iconic mountain:
- Climbing season: July-August
- View from Lake Kawaguchi
- Hakone for hot springs with Fuji views

## Essential Travel Tips

- **JR Pass**: Get a Japan Rail Pass if traveling between cities
- **Cash**: Many places still prefer cash, though cards are increasingly accepted
- **Etiquette**: Remove shoes when entering homes and some restaurants
- **Language**: Learn basic Japanese phrases, English is limited outside major cities
- **Tipping**: Not customary in Japan

## Must-Try Experiences

- Stay in a traditional ryokan (Japanese inn)
- Visit an onsen (hot spring)
- Attend a tea ceremony
- Experience a capsule hotel
- Ride the Shinkansen (bullet train)
- Try authentic sushi at Tsukiji or local sushi restaurant

## Food Highlights

- Sushi and sashimi
- Ramen (different styles in different regions)
- Tempura
- Okonomiyaki (savory pancake)
- Takoyaki (octopus balls)
- Matcha desserts

## Cultural Highlights

- Cherry blossom viewing (hanami)
- Traditional festivals (matsuri)
- Sumo wrestling tournaments
- Kabuki theater
- Zen gardens and temples
